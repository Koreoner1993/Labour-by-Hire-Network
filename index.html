About Labour by Hire

Built by the industry.
For the industry.

Labour by Hire is an Australian marketplace connecting verified tradespeople with employers. Not a job board — a construction identity network. Every tradie is licence-checked, ABN-verified, and issued a Trust Score.
The three-layer model
Layer 1
Marketplace
Tradies pay a one-time listing fee to be found. Employers pay a premium subscription to contact verified tradies. Live now.
Layer 2
Equipment Trust
Construction equipment fractionalised into shares. Token holders earn rental yield. Launching ~Month 18.
Layer 3
Property Spotlight
High-score tradies raise capital to build and sell property. CSF-compliant crowdfunding under AFSL. Launching ~Month 30.
Legal structure
Sole trader with ABN registered now. Incorporating as LBH Pty Ltd ~Month 12. AFSL application begins ~Month 24.
